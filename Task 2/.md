# 🎣 Phishing Awareness Training - Task 2

Welcome to the **Phishing Awareness Training Module**! This is an interactive web application designed to educate users on how to identify malicious actors, spot fake websites, and protect their digital assets from social engineering attacks. 

## 🌟 Features

- **Interactive Navigation:** Smooth, slide-based navigation to guide users through the training module.
- **Anatomy of a Fake Email:** Hover over specific sections of a fake email to reveal common red flags (like spoofed sender addresses, false sense of urgency, and generic greetings).
- **Social Engineering Tactics:** Learn about common attacker tactics like *False Urgency*, *Authority Spoofing*, *Baiting*, and *Spear Phishing*.
- **Best Practices:** Actionable tips to stay secure, such as verifying sources, hovering before clicking, enabling MFA, and using password managers.
- **Interactive Quiz:** Test your knowledge at the end of the module to reinforce the lessons learned!

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML5:** Semantic markup and structure for the training slides and interactive quiz.
- **CSS3:** Custom styles, animations, responsive design, and glassmorphism effects.
- **JavaScript (Vanilla):** Logic for slide transitions, hovering effects, and the interactive quiz mechanics.
- **FontAwesome:** For intuitive icons used throughout the training module.
- **Google Fonts:** Utilizing 'Inter' and 'Outfit' for a clean and modern typography experience.
